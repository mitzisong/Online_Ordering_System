About


Mitzi's Cupcakes specializes in custom cupcakes made the old-fashioned way!

Our delicious homemade treats are a festive addition to any party, special occasion or ordinary afternoon.

All of our creations are baked to order from scratch using only the finest ingredients, including fresh eggs, sweet cream butter from local dairy farms, Scharffen Berger chocolate and Nielsen-Massey pure Madagascar Bourbon vanilla.

No substitutes, no shortcuts. With extra smiles on top.
